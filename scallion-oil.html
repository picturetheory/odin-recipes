Scallion Oil (Congyou)

Description

This scallion oil is simple to make, but adds plenty of umami punch to any dish. Use it as a topping on some scallion oil noodles, or in place of regular cooking oil in your favourite dishes!

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of scallions
  • 1 small or 1/2 large onion (red, yellow, or white)
  • 2 cups of cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Chop the scallions in half. Quarter the onion.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a pot until just boiling around a wooden chopstick or spoon.
  3. Add the scallions and onion.
  4. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the scallions and onion are a deep golden brown (but not burned!).
  5. Strain the oil into a heatproof container.
