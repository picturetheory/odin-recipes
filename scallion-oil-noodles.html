Scallion Oil Noodles (Congyoumian)

Description

These scallion oil noodles are an easy weeknight meal that can be whipped up in just a few minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 serving of noodles
  • 1 teaspoon scallion oil
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the noodles in a pot according to package instructions, until al dente.
  2. While the noodles are boiling, add the scallion oil, soy sauce, and toasted sesame oil to a serving bowl. Mix gently.
  3. Drain the noodles and add to the serving bowl.
  4. Toss the noodles in the bowl until covered evenly with the sauce mixture.
  5. Top with optional sesame seeds and serve!
